## CAN for 2016-2019 vehicles (draft)

UDS commands, same RX&TX Can address. Commands are unstable, needs multiple rapid retries to get the command sent successfully to TCU.

Supported UDS diag commands:

- 0x31
- 0x2e
- 0x21
- 0x30
- 0x34
- 0x3b
- 0x32
